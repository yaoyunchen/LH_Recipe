Ingredient Quantity
Kaiser Roll 1 Roll
Butter (Melted> 1 Teaspoon
Bacon (Cooked) 1 Slice
Cheese (Your Choice) 1/2 Cup
Vegetables (Your Choice) -
Egg 1 Egg
Mushroom (Diced) 1/4 Cup
Cooking Oil 1 Teaspoon
Garlic (Diced) 1 Clove
Onion (Diced) 1/2 Cup
Salt & Pepper To Taste
Parsley To Taste

Quantity of Ingredients may change depending on what is added and size of bowl. Choice of ingredients within bowl up to cook. For vegetables, spinach and tomatos were used with avocado.

Introduction

Prep Time: 25 Minutes

Cook Time: 20 Minutes

Estimated Time: 45 Minutes

Difficulty: Easy

This is a simple and quick breadbowl meal, suitable for breakfast or brunch. It is extremely customizable, as the ingredients used can be chosen at the cook's discretion. Besides tasting great, it also looks delicious!

The bread bowl is based on a video posted by Tasty on Facebook. To view the video, click here.

For the bread bowl, you will require:

  • Oven (small convection oven will do)
  • Stove
  • Frying Pan
  • Knife
  • Spatula
  • Aluminum Foil

Procedure

  1. If using bacon
    • Heat up the frying pan and cook the bacon. When done, remove from pan and drain of oil. Slice into smaller pieces or chop into bits.
  2. If using sauteed mushrooms
    • Heat up the frying pan and a bit of cooking oil. Fry onions until brown, then add in garlic.
    • Add in the mushrooms and cook until soft.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  4. Using a knife, cut the top off the kaiser roll.
    • Only cut off around 10-15% off the top of the roll, or enough for a opening to remove the insides and add in ingredients or else the bowl will be too small or shallow.
  5. Hollow out the insides of the roll. This can be done with hands. Do not hollow out too much; the walls of the bowl shouldn't be smaller than one cm.
  6. Drizzle melted butter into the hollowed out bread roll, until the inside is well coated.
  7. Add the ingredients by layers to the bread bowl. Try to keep everything within the sides of the bowl without overflowing. For the one in the pictures:
    1. Add in a layer of cheese.
    2. Add in a layer of sliced bacon.
    3. Add in a layer of spinach and tomatoes.
    4. Add in a layer of sauteed mushrooms.
    5. Add another layer of cheese.
    6. Add an egg (raw) on top.
  8. Wrap the bread bowl with aluminum foil, keeping the top exposed. The shiny side should be pointed in towards the bowl.
  9. Place wrapped bread bowl in oven for 20 minutes, or until egg turns completely white but before it starts to turn brown.
  10. Once done, remove from oven and add garnish however you wish.
    1. Add salt and pepper
    2. Add diced avocado
    3. Add parsley
    4. Add diced tomato
  11. Enjoy!
